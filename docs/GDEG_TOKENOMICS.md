# GDEG Tokenomics Deep Dive

## Energy Balance Token (EBT)
- 1 EBT = 1 kWh verified renewable energy
- Minted by oracle on node production verification
- Burned on trade settlement or energy consumption
- No pre-mine. No inflation. Backed 1:1 by real kWh.

## GDEG Governance Token (GGT)
- Fixed supply: 100,000,000 GGT
- Community: 40M (grants, liquidity, incentives)
- Team: 30M (4yr vest, 1yr cliff)
- Investors: 20M (18mo lock)
- Reserve: 10M (emergency)
- Staking: earn 0.5% share of all platform fees
